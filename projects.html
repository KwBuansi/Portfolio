Project Gallery

Design for Dimensions

In this project, I worked in a group of three to develop a new design for Knotty & Board's Homepage and shopping page, intended for elderly users. To this end, we conducted interviews and usability tests with students at Davidson College.

Design for Understanding

This project required us to use a dataset and convert it into information easily interpretable by the untrained eye. In a team of four, I developed a webpage using d3.js that displays three visualizations: a bar chart of coffee characteristics, a choropleth map showing global coffee distribution, and a pie chart showing the percentage of coffee bags produced by each nation.

Design for Another World

This sprint involved developing an interactive space-exploration game using A-Frame. I contributed heavily to the development process, creating the environment, implementing movement and physics, and designing interactive features.

Re-Design and Extend

Our final sprint required transforming a Figma prototype into a physical kiosk system with tactile interaction. I conducted usability testing, assisted with the physical prototype, and refined our Figma designs to better support in-person shopping workflows.
